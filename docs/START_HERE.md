# Start Here: AXIS-NIDDHI in 3 Minutes

AXIS-NIDDHI is a preservation and translation project for the PureDhamma.net
teaching corpus.

The simplest way to understand it:

> AXIS-NIDDHI keeps the corpus readable as a static archive and helps make
> reviewed Portuguese access possible without silently changing meaning.

## What This Is

- A static, offline-readable archive of a large Dhamma teaching corpus.
- A controlled translation workflow with human review.
- A traceable publishing process where generated output can be compared,
  checked, and preserved.

## What This Is Not

- Not a replacement for PureDhamma.net.
- Not a new interpretation of Dhamma.
- Not an automated claim that every translation is final.
- Not a social platform or live application.

## How to Use the Public Archive

1. Open the public vitrine.
2. Enter the archive.
3. Choose a section or search for a topic.
4. Compare English and Portuguese when available.
5. Treat reviewed meaning as more important than polished wording.

Current surfaces:

- Official Netlify vitrine: `https://niddhi.netlify.app/`
- Cloudflare preview/test surface: `https://niddhi.pages.dev/welcome.html`

## Translation Status

The corpus is large. Portuguese coverage is growing in reviewed batches. Some
entries may still be English-only or awaiting review.

## Translation Note / Aviso sobre a traducao

English: Portuguese translations are offered as study and review support for
readers who are not fluent in English. They are not official, final, or
doctrinally certified translations. The English articles at PureDhamma.net
remain the primary reference, and any unclear or unusual passage should be
reported as a review point.

Português: As traduções em português são oferecidas como apoio de estudo e
revisão para leitores que não leem inglês com fluência. Elas não são traduções
oficiais, finais ou doutrinariamente certificadas. Os artigos em inglês de
PureDhamma.net permanecem como referência principal, e qualquer passagem
estranha, confusa ou contraditória deve ser tratada como ponto de revisão, não
como autoridade final.

If you see a rough phrase, the best next question is not "why is this wrong?"
but "what exact improvement would preserve the meaning better?"

## How to Help

Useful help usually falls into one of these forms:

- report a confusing translation with the page title and section;
- identify a Pali term that should be preserved or handled more carefully;
- compare a Portuguese phrase with the English source;
- suggest clearer public documentation.

AXIS-NIDDHI is careful infrastructure for continuity: static access,
traceability, and translation review in service of meaning.
