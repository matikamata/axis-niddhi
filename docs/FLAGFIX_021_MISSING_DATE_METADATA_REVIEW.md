# FlagFix 021 — Missing Date Metadata Review Box

## Status

Planning / review artifact.

## Purpose

Review publication/revision date metadata visibility in rendered AXIS-NIDDHI pages before implementing any automated correction.

## Policy

- Preserve original PureDhamma date wording when present.
- Preserve didactic colors, including blue date emphasis such as `#3366ff`.
- Do not normalize dates automatically without human review.
- Do not infer missing dates unless source provenance is explicitly available.
- Prefer review boxes / metadata audit notes over silent editorial correction.

## Initial approved observation

A page with explicit date metadata such as:

> June 10, 2016; revised January 24, 2020; July 21, 2024

is acceptable when rendered visibly near the top of the article.

The blue date styling is considered intentional/didactic and should be preserved.
